Peanut Butter Banana Bread

General Information:

High Protein Peanut Butter Banana Bread is a nutritious treat made with vanilla whey protein, PB2 peanut butter mix, and mashed bananas. Each serving provides approximately 330 calories, 30 grams of protein, 37 grams of carbohydrates and 7 grams of fat.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Serving: 4

Ingredients:

  • 30g vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup power flour or all purpose flour
  • 3/8 cup PB2 peanut butter mix
  • 1/2 cup zero-calorie sweetener
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 mashed bananas

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 320Â°F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine power flour or all-purpose flour, vanilla protein, PB2 peanut butter, zero-calorie sweetener, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add them to the dry ingredients.
  4. Add unsweetened almond milk to the mixture and stir until well combined.
  5. Mash two ripe bananas and fold them into the batter.
  6. Grease a bread pan and pour the batter into it. Optionally add extra sliced bananas on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the banana bread to cool before slicing. Optionally drizzle peanut butter mixture on top.
